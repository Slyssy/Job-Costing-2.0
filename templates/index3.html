Green Team's Job Costing App

Green Team's Job Costing App 2.0 is here!

Construction is a competitive business. With less resources, it's difficult for smaller companies to stay competitive. An owner, project manager, or project lead will need to manage many aspects of the project such as timelines, purchase orders, labor, financials,and meetings with Stakeholders for each project from bid stage to close.

We wanted to help smaller companies gain a competitive advantage by providing a user friendly project management tool with features to aid in the efficiency of labor and financial management. Get a company overview of all projects on the Project Dashboard or check an indvidual projects progress using its project details page.

This app will:

    Allow leads to asses individual projects progress by comparing important financials and labor data
    Allow individuals to update project information on the job in real time
    Allow employees or project managers to update timesheets
    Provide useful visualizations for quick analysis and reporting

Updates Include:

    Revenue Performance Visualization in D3
    Log-in/User Authentication
    D3 Projects Map
    Labor Performance Visualization in D3
